The latest 2025 car models include the Tesla Model 3 Highland, Toyota Camry Hybrid, BMW iX2, Ford Mustang GTD, and Hyundai Ioniq 6.

How often should I service my car?

You should service your car every 10,000 km or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Regular oil changes and inspections ensure longevity.
